About Us

Here at Dignans Catering we strive to deliver delicious food to all our customers. Established in 2019 the surrounding Edinburgh community have adopted us as part of their family. In addition to its charming café space, the business also operates a thriving catering service, offering bespoke menu options for events of all sizes. The catering side is known for its attention to detail, providing everything from intimate gatherings to large corporate events with expertly crafted dishes. Clients can choose from a variety of catering packages, including options for dietary restrictions and preferences, ensuring that every guest is satisfied. The team works closely with clients to create customized menus that reflect the theme and tone of the event, whether it's a wedding, a corporate meeting, or a casual get-together.

Our Chefs

At the heart of every successful cafe and catering company lies a team of exceptionally skilled chefs who bring passion, creativity, and expertise to every dish. These chefs are not only masters of their craft but are also dedicated to using the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to create menus that delight and inspire. Their ability to blend flavors, textures, and presentation elevates simple meals into memorable experiences, whether it’s a cozy breakfast for two or a grand catering event. With a deep understanding of culinary techniques and a flair for innovation, they constantly push the boundaries of flavor while respecting the traditions and heritage of classic dishes. The chefs in this cafe and catering company are known for their meticulous attention to detail and their commitment to providing guests with an unforgettable dining experience. They work collaboratively, sharing knowledge and experimenting with new ideas to ensure that every menu item is a standout.
